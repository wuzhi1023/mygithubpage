Intern Works at Karsh Hagan

I was a full time User Experience/User Interface Intern at ad agendy Karsh Hagan.
During my internship at Karsh Hagan, I worked in their UX team, not only did I learn a lot about how UX designers work in real life, they also gave me opportunities to do a lot of real world UX works to polish up my skills.
I conducted competitor research for website design, created design deliverables such as personas, userflows, clickpaths, eco-system maps, wireframes and prototypes for a number of clients, including Anschutz Health and Wellness Center, University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus Advancement Office, Colorado Mountain College, TD Ameritrade, University of Colorado Denver, Tryba Architects, etc.
