MUER

MUER means eye and ear in Chinese. It is a course project, an app for people to find and share songs that match the emotion of pictures they take.
I collaborated with teammates from art and design department, and was responsible for user experience design and prototype implementation.
We composed various personas, wireframes, storyboards, designed and implemented the user interface prototype of the app.
