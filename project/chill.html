Chill

Chill is a course project. It is an app that makes it easier to hangout with your friends. Choose who you want to hang out with and when, and Chill will take care of the rest.
  • See a feed of what your friends are up to, join them if you like, and then collectively decide on what to do, with an easy voting system.
  • Create events and make them visible to all of your friends, or a select group of them.
  • Declare yourself available to chill and get notified when any of your friends are also willing to chill.
The design process involved skills including sketches, storyboards, personas and scenarios.
We also conducted user tests and heuristic evaluation, implemented mid-fidelity prototype.
